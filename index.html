FAQ

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything you write stays on your device. OneL ine has no cloud backend, no accounts, and no analytics. Your words are yours alone.

Can I back up my journal?

You can export your full year as a Markdown, PDF, or DOCX file at any time from the Book View. We recommend doing this regularly to keep a personal backup.

What happens if I miss a day?

Nothing. You can go back and fill in any day you missed. OneL ine is designed to be forgiving — life happens.

How do I export my book?

Go to Book View, tap Export, and choose your format — Markdown, PDF, or DOCX. Your entire year exports as a single structured file.

Will my entries carry over to next year?

OneL ine is designed around a single year. Export your 2026 book at year's end, then start fresh for 2027.
